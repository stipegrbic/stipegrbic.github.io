# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 18, 2026

InCharge ("we", "our", or "us") operates the InCharge mobile application (the "App").

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

* * *

## 1\. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

InCharge allows you to create and manage responsibilities, categories, and reminders.  
All content you enter into the app (such as titles, descriptions, and dates) is stored locally on your device.

We do not require account creation.

We do not collect personal identity information such as your name, address, or phone number.

* * *

### Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription or lifetime upgrade, payments are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play.

We do not receive or store your payment details.  
We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

* * *

### Analytics (If Applicable)

InCharge may use privacy-friendly analytics tools to understand general usage patterns (such as feature usage or crashes).

This information:

*   Does not personally identify you
    
*   Is used only to improve the app experience
    

If analytics are not used, you can remove this section.

* * *

## 2\. How Your Data Is Stored

All responsibility data is stored locally on your device.

If cloud backup or synchronization features are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

* * *

## 3\. How We Use Information

We use limited information to:

*   Provide and maintain app functionality
    
*   Improve performance and reliability
    
*   Respond to support requests
    

We do not sell your data.  
We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

* * *

## 4\. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.  
You are responsible for securing access to your device.

* * *

## 5\. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including:

*   The right to request information about your data
    
*   The right to request deletion of data
    
*   The right to withdraw consent
    

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.
