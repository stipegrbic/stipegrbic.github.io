**Frequently Asked Questions**

**How do I add a car?**  
Open the Cars tab and tap "Add Car". Fill in the make, model, year, current mileage, and fuel type.

**How is the monthly cost calculated?**  
DriveCost adds all your fuel, expense, and service entries and divides by the number of months since your first entry. The more you log, the more accurate it gets.

**How is fuel consumption calculated?**  
Based on consecutive fill-ups. You need at least two fuel entries with mileage recorded for consumption to appear.

**Is my data backed up?**  
All data is stored locally on your device. We recommend using iCloud device backups to protect your data. A cloud sync feature is planned for a future update.

**How do I delete an entry?**  
Swipe left on any entry in the History screen to delete it.

**How do I delete a car?**  
Open the Cars tab, tap the car, then tap the delete icon in the top right. This will permanently delete the car and all its records.

**Can I use DriveCost for multiple cars?**  
Yes. Add as many cars as you need from the Cars tab.
