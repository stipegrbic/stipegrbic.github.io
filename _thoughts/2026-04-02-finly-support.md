---

**Common topics:**

- Restoring a Premium purchase — tap More > Upgrade to Premium > Restore purchases
- Importing transactions — use a CSV file with columns: Date, Amount, Type, Category, Notes, Tags
- Exporting your data — tap More > Export to download your transactions as CSV or Excel
- Cancelling your subscription — manage it directly in iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
