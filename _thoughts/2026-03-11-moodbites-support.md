## MoodBites — App Support

**MoodBites** is an emotional eating tracker that helps you understand the connection between your mood, food choices, and hydration. Everything is stored privately on your device.

* * *

### What MoodBites Does

**Food Log** — Log what you eat, when you eat it, the portion size, and how you were feeling. You can tag multiple moods per meal and add optional notes.

**Mood Check-ins** — Record your mood at any time and optionally tag what triggered it (stress, boredom, hunger, celebration, etc.).

**Water Tracker** — Track your daily water intake against a goal you set yourself.

**AI Advisor (Moo)** — After logging at least 2 meals or mood entries, Moo analyses your last 7 days and gives you personalised insights. You can also chat with Moo about any specific meal or your eating patterns in general.

**Craving Support** — When you feel an urge to eat emotionally, tap the red button. The app guides you through a short mindfulness moment and logs the event so you can spot patterns over time.

**Search** — Search your entire history by food name, mood, notes, meal type, portion size, or trigger.

* * *

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my data stored online?** No. All your logs (food, mood, water, cravings) are stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. The only network activity is the AI Advisor, which sends your recent logs to Claude (by Anthropic) to generate insights. No account or sign-in is required.

**How do I edit or delete a logged entry?** Tap any entry in the Food Log or Mood log to open it. From there you can edit all fields or delete the entry.

**The AI Advisor says I need more data — what should I do?** Log at least 2 meals or mood check-ins and try again. The more consistently you log, the more accurate Moo's insights will be.

**How do I set up meal or water reminders?** Go to Settings → Meal Reminders or Water Reminders. You can add up to 8 reminders each, set custom times, and name them (e.g. "Afternoon snack").

**Can I change the app language?** Yes — go to Settings → Language and choose from the supported languages.

**How do I delete all my data?** Your data is stored in the app's local storage. Deleting the app from your device will remove all data permanently.
