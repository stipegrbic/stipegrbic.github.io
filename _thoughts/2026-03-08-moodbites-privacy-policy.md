**MoodBites Privacy Policy**

Last updated: March 2026

**Overview**

MoodBites is a personal wellness app designed to help you track your meals, water intake, and emotional state. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

**Data We Collect**

MoodBites collects only the data you directly enter into the app:

*   Food log entries (meal name, meal type, mood, portion size, notes, date and time)
    
*   Mood check-in entries (mood, trigger, notes, date and time)
    
*   Water intake entries (amount, date and time)
    
*   App settings (your name, daily water goal, reminder preferences)
    

**Where Your Data Is Stored**

All data you enter is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud services. Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

**Data Sharing**

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your personal data to any third party, ever. There are no analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools in this app.

**Notifications**

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any network communication.

**Data Deletion**

You can delete individual entries at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data from your device.

**Children**

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

**Changes to This Policy**

If this policy changes in a future update, we will note the updated date at the top of this document.
